# Planning a Road Trip

## Route and Destination
- [ ] Choose destination and key stops along the way
- [ ] Map out the route and estimate total driving time
- [ ] Identify scenic detours or points of interest
- [ ] Research road conditions and construction zones
- [ ] Plan driving segments (no more than 6-8 hours per day)
- [ ] Download offline maps for areas with poor cell service

## Accommodations
- [ ] Book hotels, campgrounds, or Airbnbs for each night
- [ ] Confirm pet-friendly accommodations if traveling with pets
- [ ] Check cancellation policies
- [ ] Research overnight parking options if car camping
- [ ] Save confirmation numbers and addresses

## Vehicle Preparation
- [ ] Check oil level and schedule oil change if due
- [ ] Inspect tire pressure and tread depth
- [ ] Check coolant, brake fluid, and windshield washer fluid
- [ ] Test all lights (headlights, brake lights, turn signals)
- [ ] Verify spare tire and jack are in good condition
- [ ] Check wiper blades
- [ ] Confirm registration and insurance are current
- [ ] Pack jumper cables and emergency roadside kit

## Packing
- [ ] Cooler with drinks and snacks
- [ ] Reusable water bottles
- [ ] Phone mounts and car chargers
- [ ] Paper towels and trash bags
- [ ] First aid kit
- [ ] Sunglasses and sunscreen
- [ ] Comfortable driving shoes
- [ ] Pillows and blankets for passengers
- [ ] Entertainment (playlists, podcasts, audiobooks)
- [ ] Cash for tolls and small vendors

## Budget
- [ ] Estimate fuel costs for total mileage
- [ ] Budget for accommodations
- [ ] Budget for food and dining
- [ ] Set aside funds for activities and attractions
- [ ] Account for tolls and parking fees
- [ ] Build in a buffer for unexpected expenses

## Day Before Departure
- [ ] Fill up the gas tank
- [ ] Clean out the car and organize cargo space
- [ ] Charge all devices and portable batteries
- [ ] Share your itinerary with someone not on the trip
- [ ] Check weather forecast for the route
- [ ] Set out-of-office replies if needed
- [ ] Take out trash and perishable food at home

## During the Trip
- [ ] Take breaks every 2-3 hours to stretch
- [ ] Rotate drivers if possible
- [ ] Keep gas tank above quarter full
- [ ] Document the trip with photos and notes
- [ ] Stay flexible with timing and plans
- [ ] Check reviews before choosing roadside restaurants

## After the Trip
- [ ] Wash the car inside and out
- [ ] Check for any new vehicle issues
- [ ] Back up all photos and videos
- [ ] Write reviews for standout stops and hotels
- [ ] Settle shared expenses with travel companions
