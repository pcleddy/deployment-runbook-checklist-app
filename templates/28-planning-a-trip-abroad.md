# Planning a Trip Abroad

## Documents and Visas
- [ ] Check passport expiration (6+ months validity required)
- [ ] Renew passport if needed (allow 6-8 weeks)
- [ ] Research visa requirements for destination country
- [ ] Apply for visa or travel authorization (ESTA, eTA, ETA, etc.)
- [ ] Make photocopies of passport, visa, and ID
- [ ] Store digital copies in secure cloud storage
- [ ] Check if international driving permit is needed

## Health and Safety
- [ ] Research required and recommended vaccinations
- [ ] Schedule travel health appointment
- [ ] Fill prescriptions and pack extra supply of medications
- [ ] Get doctor's letter for any controlled medications
- [ ] Research local health risks and precautions
- [ ] Locate hospitals and clinics near your accommodation
- [ ] Register with your country's embassy travel program

## Finances
- [ ] Notify bank and credit card companies of travel dates
- [ ] Research currency and exchange rates
- [ ] Get some local currency before departure
- [ ] Confirm credit cards have no foreign transaction fees
- [ ] Set up a backup payment method
- [ ] Research tipping customs at destination
- [ ] Understand ATM availability and fees abroad

## Insurance and Coverage
- [ ] Purchase travel insurance (medical, trip cancellation, baggage)
- [ ] Verify health insurance covers international emergencies
- [ ] Check if credit card includes travel protections
- [ ] Save insurance policy numbers and emergency phone numbers
- [ ] Understand how to file a claim while abroad

## Flights and Transportation
- [ ] Book flights and select seats
- [ ] Check baggage allowance and restrictions
- [ ] Research airport transfers at destination
- [ ] Book ground transportation (trains, rental car, transfers)
- [ ] Download airline app for mobile boarding passes
- [ ] Check transit visa requirements for layover countries
- [ ] Confirm carry-on liquid and electronics rules

## Accommodation
- [ ] Book hotels or rentals for each destination
- [ ] Confirm check-in and check-out times
- [ ] Save confirmation numbers and addresses in local language
- [ ] Research neighborhood safety and proximity to transit
- [ ] Check if power adapters or voltage converters are needed

## Communication
- [ ] Research international phone plan or buy local SIM card
- [ ] Download offline maps for destination cities
- [ ] Download translation app with offline language packs
- [ ] Save emergency numbers for destination country
- [ ] Share full itinerary with family or emergency contact
- [ ] Set up VPN if traveling to countries with internet restrictions

## Packing
- [ ] Check weather forecast for travel dates
- [ ] Pack power adapter and voltage converter
- [ ] Bring universal plug adapter
- [ ] Pack medications in carry-on with prescriptions
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes
- [ ] Layers for varying climates and air conditioning
- [ ] Copies of all important documents
- [ ] Portable charger and charging cables
- [ ] Travel pillow and noise-canceling headphones
- [ ] Luggage locks (TSA-approved)

## Day Before Departure
- [ ] Check in online for flight
- [ ] Print or download boarding passes
- [ ] Confirm all reservations (flights, hotels, transfers)
- [ ] Charge all devices
- [ ] Set out-of-office replies
- [ ] Secure your home (locks, lights, mail hold)
- [ ] Pack carry-on essentials bag (snacks, entertainment, change of clothes)
- [ ] Double-check passport and travel documents are in carry-on

## While Abroad
- [ ] Keep passport and valuables in hotel safe
- [ ] Carry a photocopy of passport when out
- [ ] Stay aware of local scams and safety tips
- [ ] Keep embassy contact information handy
- [ ] Back up photos and documents regularly
- [ ] Track expenses for budget or reimbursement
- [ ] Try to learn a few phrases in the local language

## Returning Home
- [ ] Check duty-free allowances and customs rules
- [ ] Save receipts for customs declarations
- [ ] Reconfirm return flight details
- [ ] Pack souvenirs and gifts securely
- [ ] File travel insurance claims if needed
- [ ] File expense report if business travel
- [ ] Back up all photos and videos
