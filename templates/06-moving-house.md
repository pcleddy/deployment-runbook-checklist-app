# Moving House

## 4-6 Weeks Before
- [ ] Research and book moving company or rent truck
- [ ] Notify landlord or begin closing process
- [ ] Start decluttering and donating unwanted items
- [ ] Begin packing non-essential items
- [ ] Gather important documents in one place
- [ ] Order packing supplies (boxes, tape, markers)

## 2-4 Weeks Before
- [ ] File change of address with post office
- [ ] Update address with bank and financial accounts
- [ ] Transfer or set up utilities at new address
  - [ ] Electric
  - [ ] Gas
  - [ ] Water
  - [ ] Internet and cable
- [ ] Notify employer and update payroll
- [ ] Update insurance policies
- [ ] Arrange school transfers if needed

## Week Before
- [ ] Confirm moving company details
- [ ] Pack remaining items room by room
- [ ] Label all boxes clearly with room and contents
- [ ] Prepare essentials box (toiletries, chargers, snacks)
- [ ] Clean current home
- [ ] Take photos of current home condition
- [ ] Back up computer and important files

## Moving Day
- [ ] Do final walkthrough of old home
- [ ] Supervise loading of truck
- [ ] Check all rooms, closets, and storage areas
- [ ] Hand over keys to landlord or new owner
- [ ] Supervise unloading at new home
- [ ] Check inventory against moving list

## After Moving
- [ ] Unpack essentials first (kitchen, bathroom, bedroom)
- [ ] Test all utilities and appliances
- [ ] Update driver's license with new address
- [ ] Update vehicle registration
- [ ] Register to vote at new address
- [ ] Meet neighbors and explore the area
- [ ] Schedule any needed repairs or improvements
