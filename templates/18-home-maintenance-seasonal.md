# Seasonal Home Maintenance

## Spring
- [ ] Inspect roof for winter damage
- [ ] Clean gutters and downspouts
- [ ] Service air conditioning system
- [ ] Check exterior paint for peeling or damage
- [ ] Power wash deck, patio, and siding
- [ ] Test smoke and carbon monoxide detectors
- [ ] Check window and door screens
- [ ] Inspect foundation for cracks
- [ ] Fertilize lawn and garden beds

## Summer
- [ ] Check and clean dryer vent
- [ ] Inspect and clean outdoor grill
- [ ] Test sprinkler system and adjust coverage
- [ ] Trim trees and shrubs away from house
- [ ] Check caulking around windows and doors
- [ ] Inspect deck or patio for loose boards
- [ ] Clean and organize garage
- [ ] Check for pest entry points
- [ ] Service lawn mower and outdoor equipment

## Fall
- [ ] Schedule furnace inspection and tune-up
- [ ] Clean chimney and fireplace
- [ ] Reverse ceiling fan direction
- [ ] Drain and store garden hoses
- [ ] Winterize sprinkler system
- [ ] Clean gutters after leaves fall
- [ ] Seal gaps around doors and windows
- [ ] Stock winter supplies (salt, shovels)
- [ ] Aerate and overseed lawn

## Winter
- [ ] Check for ice dams on roof
- [ ] Keep walkways and driveways clear of ice
- [ ] Check insulation in attic and crawl spaces
- [ ] Monitor pipes for freezing risk
- [ ] Test backup sump pump
- [ ] Replace HVAC filters
- [ ] Check for drafts around windows
- [ ] Inspect water heater for leaks or sediment

## Anytime
- [ ] Replace burned-out light bulbs
- [ ] Check fire extinguisher expiration dates
- [ ] Clean range hood filters
- [ ] Unclog slow drains
- [ ] Touch up interior paint
- [ ] Organize closets and storage areas
