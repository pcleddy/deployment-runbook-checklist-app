# Selling a House

## Preparation
- [ ] Research comparable home sales in your area
- [ ] Interview and hire a real estate agent
- [ ] Get a pre-listing home inspection
- [ ] Determine asking price with agent
- [ ] Review and sign listing agreement
- [ ] Order title search and gather property documents

## Home Improvements
- [ ] Declutter and depersonalize every room
- [ ] Deep clean entire house including carpets and windows
- [ ] Touch up paint on walls and trim
- [ ] Fix minor repairs (leaky faucets, squeaky doors, broken fixtures)
- [ ] Improve curb appeal (landscaping, front door, walkway)
- [ ] Stage key rooms or hire a professional stager
- [ ] Replace burned-out light bulbs and update outdated fixtures

## Listing and Marketing
- [ ] Hire professional photographer for listing photos
- [ ] Write compelling listing description
- [ ] List on MLS and major real estate sites
- [ ] Create virtual tour or video walkthrough
- [ ] Install yard sign and lockbox
- [ ] Share listing on social media
- [ ] Schedule open house dates

## Showings
- [ ] Keep house clean and show-ready at all times
- [ ] Remove valuables and sensitive documents during showings
- [ ] Arrange temporary plans for pets during showings
- [ ] Leave lights on and set comfortable temperature
- [ ] Collect and review showing feedback with agent
- [ ] Adjust strategy based on feedback if needed

## Offers and Negotiation
- [ ] Review each offer with agent (price, contingencies, timeline)
- [ ] Counter or accept the best offer
- [ ] Negotiate inspection repair requests
- [ ] Review buyer's financing and pre-approval
- [ ] Agree on closing date and terms
- [ ] Sign purchase agreement

## Under Contract
- [ ] Cooperate with buyer's home inspection
- [ ] Provide required seller disclosures
- [ ] Respond to appraisal results
- [ ] Resolve any title issues
- [ ] Begin packing and planning your move
- [ ] Schedule utility transfers and mail forwarding

## Closing
- [ ] Do final walkthrough with buyer (or buyer's agent)
- [ ] Review closing documents and settlement statement
- [ ] Sign all closing paperwork
- [ ] Hand over keys, garage openers, and alarm codes
- [ ] Confirm funds received from sale
- [ ] Cancel homeowner's insurance after closing
- [ ] Save all closing documents for tax records
