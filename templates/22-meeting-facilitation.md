# Meeting Facilitation

## Before the Meeting
- [ ] Define meeting purpose and desired outcomes
- [ ] Create and share agenda in advance
- [ ] Invite only necessary participants
- [ ] Assign roles (facilitator, note-taker, timekeeper)
- [ ] Book room and check AV equipment
- [ ] Share pre-read materials at least 24 hours ahead
- [ ] Set meeting duration (default to 25 or 50 minutes)

## Opening (First 5 Minutes)
- [ ] Start on time
- [ ] State the meeting purpose and goals
- [ ] Review the agenda
- [ ] Confirm note-taker and timekeeper
- [ ] Set ground rules if needed
  - [ ] One speaker at a time
  - [ ] Phones on silent
  - [ ] Parking lot for off-topic items

## During the Meeting
- [ ] Keep discussion focused on agenda items
- [ ] Manage time across topics
- [ ] Encourage participation from quiet attendees
- [ ] Redirect off-topic conversations to parking lot
- [ ] Summarize key points after each agenda item
- [ ] Capture decisions and action items in real time
- [ ] Check for alignment before moving on

## Decision Making
- [ ] Clearly state the decision to be made
- [ ] Hear relevant perspectives
- [ ] Identify options and trade-offs
- [ ] Reach consensus or use agreed decision method
- [ ] Document the decision and rationale

## Closing (Last 5 Minutes)
- [ ] Recap decisions made
- [ ] Review action items with owners and deadlines
- [ ] Confirm next steps
- [ ] Schedule follow-up meeting if needed
- [ ] Thank participants
- [ ] End on time

## After the Meeting
- [ ] Distribute meeting notes within 24 hours
- [ ] Follow up on action items
- [ ] Address parking lot items
- [ ] Gather feedback if it was a recurring meeting
- [ ] Cancel future meetings if goals are achieved
