# Emergency Preparedness

## Emergency Kit
- [ ] Water (1 gallon per person per day for 3 days)
- [ ] Non-perishable food (3-day supply)
- [ ] Manual can opener
- [ ] Flashlight and extra batteries
- [ ] First aid kit
- [ ] Medications (7-day supply)
- [ ] Multi-tool or Swiss army knife
- [ ] Phone charger and portable battery pack
- [ ] Cash in small denominations
- [ ] Copies of important documents in waterproof bag

## Communication Plan
- [ ] Designate out-of-area emergency contact
- [ ] Ensure all family members have contact list
- [ ] Program ICE (In Case of Emergency) contacts in phones
- [ ] Agree on meeting locations
  - [ ] Near your home
  - [ ] Outside your neighborhood
- [ ] Know how to send text messages (networks may be overloaded)
- [ ] Download emergency alert apps

## Home Preparation
- [ ] Know how to shut off gas, water, and electricity
- [ ] Secure heavy furniture and appliances to walls
- [ ] Install smoke and carbon monoxide detectors on every floor
- [ ] Store fire extinguisher in accessible location
- [ ] Identify safe spots in each room (earthquake, tornado)
- [ ] Clear emergency exit routes
- [ ] Label utility shutoff locations

## Important Documents
- [ ] Insurance policies (home, auto, health, life)
- [ ] Identification (passports, birth certificates, licenses)
- [ ] Financial records (bank accounts, investment accounts)
- [ ] Medical records and prescriptions
- [ ] Property deeds and vehicle titles
- [ ] Store digital copies in secure cloud storage

## Review and Maintain
- [ ] Check and rotate emergency food and water every 6 months
- [ ] Update medications in kit
- [ ] Replace expired batteries
- [ ] Review and practice evacuation routes
- [ ] Update emergency contacts
- [ ] Practice emergency drills with family
