# Car Buying

## Research
- [ ] Determine budget (purchase price, insurance, maintenance)
- [ ] Decide new vs. used
- [ ] Research vehicle types that fit your needs
- [ ] Compare models and read reviews
- [ ] Check safety ratings and reliability data
- [ ] Research current incentives and rebates

## Financing
- [ ] Check your credit score
- [ ] Get pre-approved for auto loan from bank or credit union
- [ ] Compare dealer financing vs. outside lender rates
- [ ] Calculate monthly payment you can afford
- [ ] Understand loan terms (length, interest, down payment)
- [ ] Review gap insurance options

## Shopping
- [ ] Visit dealerships and test drive top choices
- [ ] Check vehicle history report (used cars)
- [ ] Inspect exterior, interior, tires, and engine
- [ ] Verify VIN and title are clean
- [ ] Compare prices across multiple dealers
- [ ] Check online inventory for best deals
- [ ] Get quotes from at least 3 sources

## Negotiation
- [ ] Know the fair market value before negotiating
- [ ] Negotiate purchase price before discussing trade-in
- [ ] Get trade-in appraised at multiple locations
- [ ] Review and question all fees and add-ons
- [ ] Decline unnecessary extras (extended warranty, paint protection)
- [ ] Get final offer in writing

## Purchase
- [ ] Review all contract terms carefully
- [ ] Verify VIN on paperwork matches vehicle
- [ ] Confirm warranty coverage and terms
- [ ] Arrange insurance before driving off lot
- [ ] Complete title and registration paperwork
- [ ] Get copies of all signed documents

## After Purchase
- [ ] Register vehicle with DMV
- [ ] Set up maintenance schedule
- [ ] Program garage opener and Bluetooth
- [ ] Add vehicle to insurance policy
- [ ] Save all documentation and receipts
- [ ] Schedule first service appointment
