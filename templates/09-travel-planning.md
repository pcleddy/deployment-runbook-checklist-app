# Travel Planning

## Booking (4+ Weeks Out)
- [ ] Research destination and best time to visit
- [ ] Book flights or transportation
- [ ] Book accommodation
- [ ] Check passport validity (6+ months remaining)
- [ ] Apply for visa if required
- [ ] Purchase travel insurance

## Preparation (1-2 Weeks Out)
- [ ] Research local customs and etiquette
- [ ] Download offline maps and translation apps
- [ ] Notify bank of travel dates
- [ ] Check phone plan for international coverage
- [ ] Make copies of important documents
  - [ ] Passport
  - [ ] Travel insurance
  - [ ] Hotel confirmations
  - [ ] Flight itineraries
- [ ] Arrange pet care or house sitter
- [ ] Set up out-of-office replies

## Packing
- [ ] Check weather forecast for destination
- [ ] Pack clothing appropriate for activities
- [ ] Toiletries and medications
- [ ] Chargers and power adapters
- [ ] Travel documents and ID
- [ ] Snacks and water bottle
- [ ] Entertainment for transit (books, headphones)
- [ ] Day bag or backpack

## Day Before Departure
- [ ] Check in online for flights
- [ ] Confirm hotel and car rental reservations
- [ ] Charge all devices
- [ ] Set home security system
- [ ] Take out trash and perishable food
- [ ] Confirm airport transportation

## During Travel
- [ ] Keep important documents accessible
- [ ] Stay hydrated and adjust to time zone
- [ ] Check in with emergency contact
- [ ] Back up photos regularly
- [ ] Keep receipts for business expenses

## Return
- [ ] Unpack and do laundry
- [ ] File expense report if business travel
- [ ] Back up all photos and videos
- [ ] Write reviews for hotels and restaurants
- [ ] Update travel journal or blog
